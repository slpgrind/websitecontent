Yao Du

Yao Du is a PhD student in Informatics (Fall 2016-Present) at University of California, Irvine. Her research focuses on designing and evaluating speech and language assessment for young children on the mobile platform. She holds a M.A. in Communication Science and Disorders from University of Texas, Austin and has previously worked as a speech language pathologist with both pediatric and geriatric population across multiple settings such as schools and hospitals.

Current Projects

Time for Children: a mobile game for receptive language screening among children 2-4 years old

Storytime: an avatar-mediated assessment for assessing narrative skills among children 4-6 years old

Tools and Skills

Design: Prototyping and Wireframing (Sketch and Invision)

Research: Surveys, Interviews, Usability Studies
